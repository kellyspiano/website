Kelly's Piano is located at Pacific Pines on the Gold Coast, Queensland. Lessons are offered in a home environment. I currently teach on a Kawai CN41, a full size digital piano with fully weighted keys and an authentic acoustic piano playing experience. A digital piano allows for the option of recording a student's piece, either for playback to friends and family or as an opportunity to critque and learn.

Parents are welcome to remain throughout the lesson, with lounge facilities available within the vicinity of the piano.

I adapt my teaching style to suit the learning style of the student, ranging from structured workbooks through to using current popular music as a means to learn new styles and skills. It is my strong belief though that learning the piano is much the same as learning any other instrument or skill: to be successful, regular practise is vital.

Terms and conditions - Tutoring

  • Normal teaching terms are as per the Queensland School Holiday timetable, though lessons can be arranged outside of these times where applicable.
  • 24 hours notice is required for all cancellations or the lesson is forfeited.
  • Where adequate notice is given, I will accomodate two cancelled lessons per term with make up lessons at a mutually convenient time.
  • Unfortunately, make-up lessons cannot be rescheduled or rolled into the next term.
  • No lessons will occur on gazetted public holidays and students will not be billed for a class that falls on a public holiday.
