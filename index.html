Kelly Coomber has been playing the piano for 22 years, studying under Narelle Flanagan for some of those. Having completed examinations through the Trinity College London and the Australian Music Examinations Board in both Pianoforte and the Theory of Music, Kelly has accompanied many bands and choirs in both performance and competition environments.

More recently, Kelly uses her skills to compose original works that focus on the emotive side of music. Kelly is qualified to teach beginner to intermediate piano and piano for leisure.
