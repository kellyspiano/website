Hello! My name is Kelly Coomber. I live in Pacific Pines and I can teach you or your children to play the piano.

I have been playing the piano for 22 years, studying privately under Narelle Flanagan for some of those. I have completed examinations through the Trinity College London and the Australian Music Examinations Board in both Pianoforte and the Theory of Music. I have also used my skills to accompany many bands, choirs and soloists in both leisure performance and competition environments.

More recently, my passion has evolved to composing original works that focus on the emotive side of music. Some of my original works, along with other performances are available in the media section. In addition to the piano, I have previously studied the flute, bassoon and percussion instruments both privately and through the public education system on the Gold Coast. I was the inaugral recipient of the Palm Beach Currumbin State High School Musical scholarship and graduated from there, receiving the annual award for Excellence in Performing Arts.

I am qualified to teach beginner to intermediate piano and piano for leisure along with offering tuition for early grades of Theory of Music. I hold a Blue Card, issued by the Commission for Children, Young People and Child Guardian, Queensland valid until the end of 2013.
