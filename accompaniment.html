I am available to provide accompaniment for soloists, small ensembles, choirs and larger bands for Eisteddfods', auditions, examinations and other performances. This extends to private groups and both public and private education bodies.

In addition to accompaniment, I am also able to produce digital rehearsal recordings to enable practice outside of rehearsal times.

Practice and performance packages can be arranged. Please contact me directly to discuss options and pricing in this regard.

The fee scale for accompaniment is as follows:
  Rate
Performance and Practice session $150
Performance and Rehearsal tape recording $125
Recording accompaniment only (per recording) $50

Terms and conditions - Accompaniment

  • I require a minimum of four weeks notice for accompaniment to ensure availability and allow adequate preparation.
  • Piano score sheet music must be provided and is the sole responsibility of the performer.
